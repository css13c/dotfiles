Personal Keybinding Cheat Sheet
---------------------------------

Vim:
	<C-e> - Scroll Page Up
	<C-y> - Scroll Page Down
	Search Functionality:(:%s)
		- Whitespace:
			- Tabs '\t'
			- Newline '\n'
			- Carraige Return '\r'
			- Space ' '
			- Any Whitespace '\s'

VsVim:
	<leader>da - Attach to Process
	<leader>db - Toggle Breakpoint on Line
	<leader>ec - Comment selection (visual mode)
	<leader>eu - Uncomment selection (visual mode)
